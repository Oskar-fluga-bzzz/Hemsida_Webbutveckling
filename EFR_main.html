Info

Welcome to Tax Fraud Studios! We’re a small indie game company who specialize in creating unique and innovative games that push the boundaries of gaming. Our team is comprised of experienced professionals who are passionate about creating games that are both entertaining and thought-provoking. At Tax Fraud Studios, we don’t just make games – we make art. Our games are created with a unique vision that challenges the status quo and pushes the envelope. We strive to create games that are both entertaining and subversive, offering an alternative perspective on the world we live in. But that’s not all – we also have a commitment to tax evasion. Our team of financial wizards have been trained to bend the rules and exploit loopholes in order to maximize profits for our investors. So if you’re looking to make a quick buck, you’ve come to the right place! So pull up a chair, grab a controller and join the world of Tax Fraud Studios. We guarantee you won’t be disappointed!

Our Next Game

Introducing Escape From Rimbo, the newest game from Tax Fraud Studios! This action-packed, open-world adventure puts you in the shoes of a daring hero who must escape from the dystopian city of Rimbo. You’ll need to use your wits and cunning to outwit the authorities, outrun the police and evade the law. Along the way, you’ll encounter a variety of characters, from corrupt politicians to dangerous criminals. You’ll also need to use the power of tax evasion to your advantage, exploiting loopholes and finding new ways to maximize your profits. Escape From Rimbo is the perfect game for any fan of subversive, open-world adventures. So if you’re looking for an immersive and thrilling experience, you’ve come to the right place! Join us in the world of Tax Fraud Studios and experience the ultimate escape.
